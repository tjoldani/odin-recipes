Peanut Butter Noodels

Description

These noodles are vegan, gluten free, and only take about 20 minutes to prepare. You can add protiens like chicken or tofu as well.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces brown ride noodles
  • 3 large cloves of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon fresh grated ginger
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2/3 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 4 tablespoosns low soduam tumari or gluten free soy sauce
  • 1/3-1 cup hot water
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
  • 1-2 tablespoons maple syrup or honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Boil noodles according to package instructions.
  2. Mince garlic and add to pan with 1 Tbsp of olive oil.
  3. Saute over low heat for about 2 minutes or until golden brown.
  4. Add the grated ginger and saute for 1 more minute.
  5. Remove from heat and add 1/3 cup of water (more if needed), and whisk in the remaining liquids until smooth.
  6. Add the suace to pasta.
  7. Serve with crushed peanuts, scallions, and red pepper flakes.
