Frijoles de Olla

Description

A very simple and traditional recipes for frijoles de olla that you can eat straight from the pot or to meal prep throughtout the week.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound (225 g) dry beans, such as pinto, California pink, or black turtle
  • 1/4 white onion, roughly sliced
  • 1 heaped tablespoon lard or olive oil
  • 1 Tbs chicken boullion
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Run the beans through your hands to pick out any small stones or pieces of earth.
  2. Rinse twice in cold water and drain.
  3. Put in a pot and cover with at least 3 inches of water.
  4. Add the onion and lard and bring to a simmer.
  5. Continue to simmer until the bean skins are soft, then add the salt, boullion, and continue cooking until the beans are very soft and the broth is soupy.
